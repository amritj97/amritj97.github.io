Work About Resume

Love By Didi

Co-founder, Present

I've always been interested in weddings, and stationary is a big part of wedding culture. Being Indo-Canadian, I found that it was hard to identify with a lot of the stationary in the current market. I started Love By Didi with my sister to fill that need.

GETTING STARTED

I have been a bridesmaid over 4 times so far and I've witnessed the struggle of finding the perfect wedding stationary over and over. Indian weddings are complex, but wedding stationary shouldn't have to be.

CURRENT CLIENTS

So far, I've worked with a total of 12 clients to design the perfect wedding stationary. This involves meeting with brides, understanding the theme of their events and building branding from the ground up for their event.

CHRISTMAS MARKETS

For the last two years, we have also participated in various Chrismtas Markets in Toronto, and donated the money to various charities.

EXPANSION AND NEXT STEPS

So far, we have been working with clients one on one to provide the best experience as possible. We are currently working on creating 'sets' for each season as the wedding trends evolve. We are also currently working on a line of spices to be used for wedding gifts.
